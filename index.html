Rescue, Rehabilitate, Rehome

A community-driven platform connecting volunteers, pet parents, and veterinarians to help animals in need.

Our Mission

Animals play a vital role in the ecological balance and emotional well-being of humans. However, stray, injured, and abandoned animals often suffer due to a lack of proper care, medical attention, and safe shelter.

Rescued Souls is a community-driven platform designed to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome such animals, ensuring they receive the care and support they deserve.

Key Objectives

  • Provide a platform for volunteers to report and coordinate rescues
  • Facilitate medical care and rehabilitation through vets and shelters
  • Create a database of rescued animals
  • Raise awareness and encourage community participation

Platform Features

Volunteer Module

  • Report stray/injured animals with photos and locations
  • Update rescue status in real-time
  • Coordinate with nearby shelters and vets

Pet Parent Module

  • Browse pets information and adoption options
  • Request vet appointments
  • Chat with other pet parents and volunteers
  • Rent a companion program

Vet/Shelter Module

  • Update medical reports and vaccination details
  • List available space for rescued animals
  • Participate in rent a companion program
